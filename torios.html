JWM is written in C and uses only Xlib at a minimum. Because of its small footprint, JWM makes a good window man- ager for older computers and less powerful systems, such as the Raspberry Pi, though it is perfectly capable of running on mod- ern systems. JWM is included in small Linux distributions such as Puppy Linux and Damn Small Linux, and it is available as a separate package in many other distributions. http://www.joewing.net/projects/jwm/ Torios jwm development lead Israel israel@torios.org 15 16 CHAPTER 5.
